Project Title: Smart Rain Drop Sensor for Electrical Pillar Protection

Description: The Smart Rain Drop Sensor project is designed to enhance safety by detecting rainfall and preventing water-related hazards in public electrical systems. The sensor is placed near electrical pillars or installations to monitor rain drops. When water is detected, the system automatically triggers protective measures such as covering the pillar or disconnecting the power supply to avoid short circuits, fire accidents, or damage to electrical infrastructure. This project is an embedded system that integrates rain drop sensing technology with a microcontroller for real-time monitoring and control. It ensures public safety, reduces maintenance costs, and minimizes risks caused by rainwater exposure to electrical equipment.

Key Features:
Detects rainfall in real-time using a rain drop sensor.
Automatically activates protective mechanisms for electrical pillars.
Prevents short circuits, fire hazards, and equipment damage.
Cost-effective and energy-efficient solution for urban safety.
Can be scaled for use in smart city infrastructure.
